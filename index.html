Our Mission

To educate and inform the Government, Health care Professionals and and the wider public.

ZikaJA Strives to provide credible information, support communities, and offer in-depth reference material about Zika related subjects that matter to you. We are a source for original and timely health information, as well as material from well-known content providers..
